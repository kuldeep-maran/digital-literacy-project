## Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
- Always verify website URLs before entering details
- Never share OTP, passwords, or bank details
- Use strong and unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
- Regularly update your apps and software
- Check sender email address carefully before responding
- For UPI safety: Do not approve unknown payment requests
- For UPI safety: Always double-check receiver details before sending money

### Report Cybercrime
